Reviews











Seth Baird

Wow Trajan's Market was such a cool place to visit! I can't help but imagine what it must have been like back then. I think marketplaces have a really cool atmosphere. If I were there back in 107-110 AD I would have loved to go to experience the busyness and loudness going on in the market. I also would have loved to see what each of the merchants were selling and probably buy some of it myself. It would probably be something that I would do after work or something on the weekend, a fun trip to the market.
Mercatus Traiani est. 107-110 AD
